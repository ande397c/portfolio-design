Introduction

The assignment was to apply a both a light theme and dark theme color pallet to a mockup landing page design, then add animations. The overall solution is a combination of the previous assignment and the use of animations. The assignment taught me more when it comes to creating color palettes and the use of fonts. For both themes, I decided to use darker colors.

Combining fonts, colors and animations

I used Adobe's color-contrast-analyzer. This helped me validate if the contrast was high enough. This was also used when creating the dark theme. Adding animations helped emphasize the overall design of the assignment. The animations are not completely over the top and are instead more subtle.
